# 11 kV to 33 kV with Umax 36 kV

## Public or private land and transformer termination type must be verified. If necessary, also confirm distribution network operator specifications and local regulations such as BS 7870 4.10.

MV cables are well established but physical parameters must still be agreed with cable and accessories suppliers. There is a general capacity issue and lead time must be planned well in advance to secure a solution.
