Technical Notes & Standards Compliance
 * Aluminium Size Restriction: According to IEC 60228 Clause 5.2.1 (b), stranded aluminium or aluminium alloy conductors must have a nominal cross-sectional area of not less than 10 mm².
 * Reference Temperature: All base resistance values are maximum values defined and tested at a base temperature of 20°C.
 * Field Measurement Correction: To correct d.c. resistance measured at temperature t back to the standard 20°C, the formula from Annex A applies:
   
   * k_t is the temperature correction factor (refer to Table A.1 in the standard).
   * L is the length of the cable in meters.
 * Operating Temperature (90°C): These calculated values represent the conductor under full load conditions for typical XLPE-insulated power cables.
