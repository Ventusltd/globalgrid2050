## Weight Formulas & Pricing Rule

- **Copper kg per km:** Area (mm²) × 9.6
- **Aluminium kg per km:** Area (mm²) × 2.92
- **Net Price:** Metal value ÷ 0.30 (Assuming raw metal constitutes 30% of the final delivered cost)
