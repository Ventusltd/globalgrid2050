## ⚠️ Technical Compliance & Safety Notice
* **Tight Bend Radii:** The manufacturer **MUST** be consulted for applications requiring tight bends to ensure structural integrity and prevent jacket failure.
* **Thermal Management:** For installations involving numerous circuits, professional **Power Systems Specialists** must be engaged to perform thermal modeling. Incorrect cable configuration can lead to **substation thermal runaway**.
* **Margin for Error:** At high currents, the margin for error is minimal. Bespoke thermal, mechanical, civil, and electrical considerations must be negotiated within the **Employer’s Requirements** for full indemnification.
