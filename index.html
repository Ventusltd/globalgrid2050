# GlobalGrid2050 An open grid development, engineering, procurement, construction and operations platform dedicated to documenting, analysing and improving the world's electrical energy systems as they undergo rapid electrification. The transition towards solar power, energy storage and expanded electrical infrastructure will reshape the operation of modern power networks and requires careful engineering understanding. The project aims to build a publicly accessible body of knowledge focused on the physical behaviour of electrical systems, grid stability, solar generation, storage integration, transmission infrastructure and electrical safety.
