# 06 Commercial Engineering Interface

## Core idea

V6 gives commercial people access to knowledge that was previously beyond them.

It does not turn finance, sales, development or investment teams into engineers.

It gives them a disciplined interface into engineering reality before major commercial decisions are made.

## Why this matters

In real solar, storage and grid projects, commercial decisions often move faster than engineering clarity.

Land interest, grid applications, investor conversations, EPC expectations, cable cost assumptions, revenue assumptions and development valuations can become locked in before the electrical implications are properly understood.

V6 is intended to reduce that gap.

## What V6 gives non-engineers

```text
grid voltage class visibility
substation context
transmission corridor context
solar and BESS topology awareness
cable route awareness
loss awareness
CAPEX sensitivity
revenue sensitivity
site scale intuition
engineering assumption visibility
```

## What V6 should help commercial users ask

```text
Is this land near serious grid infrastructure?
Is the site only near local distribution assets?
What voltage class is nearby?
What export route may be realistic?
How does cable length affect losses and cost?
How does topology affect CAPEX?
How does BESS change land, grid and revenue logic?
Which assumptions are missing?
Which studies are required next?
```

## What V6 must not imply

```text
confirmed grid headroom
connection approval
final cable design
formal load flow
protection grading
MCCB or ACB selection
EPC price certainty
investment advice
bankable technical due diligence
```

## Commercial value

V6 moves engineering thinking earlier in the commercial process.

It lets finance and sales teams see enough structure to avoid blind optimism, weak assumptions and avoidable project risk.

The value is not that it replaces engineers.

The value is that it makes commercial users better prepared before they speak to engineers, grid specialists, EPCs, investors and land teams.

## Doctrine line

```text
V6 gives commercial people access to engineering knowledge that was previously beyond them, without pretending to replace formal engineering judgement.
```
