# PV Inverter Installed Base and Market Structure (End 2025)

## PV Inverter Market Split

The solar inverter market is currently close to balanced between central and string architectures.

Central inverters represent about **50 to 55 percent of installed utility scale capacity**, particularly in very large plants.

String inverters account for roughly **45 to 50 percent and are gaining share in new deployments**.

The shift toward string systems is driven by modular design, redundancy and simpler installation.

Microinverters remain a small segment mainly used in residential solar systems.
