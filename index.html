GlobalGrid2050

GlobalGrid2050 is an open engineering and operations platform dedicated to the analysis of global electrical systems during rapid electrification. As infrastructure shifts toward solar generation and storage, this project provides a public body of knowledge focused on grid stability, physical system behavior, and transmission safety.
